Rules

Chatting Rules

  • Using the N-word or C-word is against the rules and it will result to a ban.
  • There will be no sexual talk allowed in this server.
  • You may just swear a bit, but do not do it often.
  • There will be no bullying allowed.
  • Respect every member in the server.
  • NSFW objects are not allowed.
  • Emote spam (more than 5 emotes in a row) and caps spam (more than 10 characters with caps lock) are not allowed.
  • Use the channels in their respective way (e.g. english chat in #》general-english or bot commands in #】bot-command).

Tagging Rules

  • Do not tag me unless it is important.
  • If you have questions regarding the server, ask those to the admins and moderators.
  • Ghost pings are not allowed.
  • THE ULTIMATE SIN: Do not use the @everyone tag as it annoys me and everyone.

Personal Rules

  • If you are under 13, please leave this server. If you don't leave, the staff and I will force you to leave this server via ban.
  • Your profile picture and username should not contain any NSFW content or racial, sexual, and bad words.
  • Alternate accounts are not allowed unless there is something wrong with your main account.
  • Impersonating is not allowed.

Penalties

  • If you broke one or more rules for the first time, you will get a warn.
  • If you broke one or more rules for the second time, you will get a mute for 10 minutes.
  • If you broke one or more rules for the third time, you will get a mute for 30 minutes.
  • If you broke one or more rules for the fourth time, you will get banned.
  • If you report someone about their doings which is not true, you will be muted for 1 hour.
